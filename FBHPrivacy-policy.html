Privacy Policy — Fatebound Heroes

Last updated: 2026-04-19

Fatebound Heroes ("the app") is published by Zari Apps ("we", "us"). This policy explains what information the app collects, why, and the choices you have.

TL;DR

  • The app does not require you to create an account.
  • All your game progress is stored on your device.
  • We do not operate our own servers and do not collect analytics directly.
  • The app displays ads via Google AdMob and offers a one-time "Remove Ads" in-app purchase through Google Play Billing. These Google services collect their own data under their own policies.

Information we collect

We (Zari Apps) do not collect, sell, or share personal data directly. The app itself runs locally on your device.

Information Google's services collect on our behalf

Google AdMob (ads)

To show ads, Google's AdMob SDK may collect information such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • Approximate (coarse) location derived from your IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • Ad interaction events (impressions, clicks)

Google uses this data to serve and measure ads, including personalised ads where applicable law permits. Google's practices:

  • policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543 (data collected by AdMob)

You can reset or limit your Advertising ID in your device's system settings ("Ads" or "Privacy" section).

Google Play Billing (in-app purchases)

When you purchase "Remove Ads", the transaction is handled entirely by Google Play Billing. We receive only a confirmation that the entitlement was granted. We never see your payment details. See policies.google.com/privacy.

Children

The app contains cartoon fantasy combat and is not directed at children under 13. If we learn we have inadvertently collected data from a child in a manner that violates applicable law, we will delete it.

Your choices

  • Uninstall the app to erase all local game data from your device.
  • Buy "Remove Ads" to stop interstitial ads. Rewarded ads are always opt-in — they only play if you tap a reward button.
  • Reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalised ads in your device system settings.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes we will update the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
