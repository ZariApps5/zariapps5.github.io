PRIVACY POLICY  ·  LAST UPDATED APRIL 17, 2026

Overview

DnDice ("the App") is a dice-rolling utility for tabletop RPG players. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the App and how it is used.

Information We Collect

DnDice itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require an account, registration, or login of any kind. All dice rolls and preferences are stored locally on your device only.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. As part of delivering ads, Google may automatically collect certain information, which may include:

  • Your device's advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and general location
  • Device model, OS version, and language settings
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's own Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device's settings under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party SDK:

  • Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob) – for displaying banner ads. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can address it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
