# Privacy Policy — Omni: Infinite RPG

_Last updated: 2026-04-24_

This Privacy Policy explains how the Omni: Infinite RPG mobile game ("the Game", "we", "us") handles information when you play. By installing or using the Game, you agree to the practices described below.

## 1. Information we collect

**Account data.** When you start the Game we create an anonymous Firebase user ID. This ID is unique to your install and is not tied to your name, email, or phone number unless you later choose to link a Google or Apple account. Linking is optional and used only to sync your progress across devices.

**Gameplay data.** Your character progress, inventory, gold, gems, completed levels, and similar in-game state are stored on Firebase Cloud Firestore so you can resume on any device after linking an account.

**Diagnostics & analytics.** We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand how the Game is played and to fix bugs. These services collect:
- Approximate session length, screen views, and in-game events (e.g. "skirmish completed", "level up")
- Device model, OS version, app version, language, region
- Stack traces and device state if the app crashes
- A randomly-generated install instance ID

We do **not** collect your real name, email, address, contacts, photos, microphone, or precise location.

**Advertising.** The Game shows ads delivered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect your Advertising ID, IP address, and basic device info to serve and measure ads. You can reset or limit your Advertising ID in your device settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads).

## 2. How we use the information

- Run, save, and sync your gameplay
- Detect and fix crashes and gameplay bugs
- Measure overall game health (retention, churn, balance)
- Show ads and pay developers via AdMob

We do not sell your information.

## 3. Children's privacy

The Game is intended for ages 13 and up. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the Game, contact us at the email below and we will delete the associated data.

## 4. Data retention

Anonymous account data is kept while your install is active. If you uninstall, the anonymous account remains in our database but cannot be linked back to you. Linked accounts are kept until you request deletion.

## 5. Your rights

You may request a copy or deletion of your account data by emailing us at the address below. Provide your in-game user ID (visible in Settings) so we can locate your account.
